About Cinematography

Cinematography is an essential part of filmmaking. It's about creative visuals that reinforce a narrative and give the audience an emotional experience. It's less about the equipment you use and more about how you use it, but this fact is often overlooked by contemporary filmmakers. It also involves a crucial part which is "storytelling" and without storytelling, one would just be taking random pictures or creating videos without meaning

My story

It all started at Digify Nigeria when we had a class on photography and how to take great pictures. Prior to then, I already had a flare for the camera Mrs Victoria Veca. This class just brought alive the spark again and I found myself taking more professional images just like the ones you can see right beside this write-up.

My story cont'd

It grew to the point where I had to get real-time serious with it at my current workplace and I had to schedule and execute video shoot sessions directly with influencers and cover major events. It was at this point I understood the power of storytelling. Even the simplest of images and short videos, all have inspiration and emotions wrapped around them.
